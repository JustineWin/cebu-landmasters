Base Line Center Showroom Opens

October 12, 2015 / Signing of Contract for New Projects

BASE LINE CENTER, CEBU– Cebu Landmasters, Inc. exciting new upgrade to the gathering place that Cebuanos have loved for decades, is making every urban dweller’s dream of comfort city living come true with the opening of its Showroom.

Four model units are now ready for its clients to see and experience as Cebu Landmasters, Inc. unveiled to the public the grand showroom of Base Line Center in a private gathering attended by special guests and friends in the media last July 27, 2016. The units showcase the feeling of prime living right at the heart of the city with its premium finish and modern designs. These include a Deliverable Studio Unit, 2 Combined Studio Units, 1 Bedroom with Balcony and 1 Bedroom Garden Loft Unit.

Cebu Landmasters’s Chief Executive Officer Jose Soberano III, Executive Vice President Marose Soberano, and Chief Operating Officer Franco Soberano, spearheaded the unveiling of the showroom. Together with the three executives are Cebu City Councilor Hon. Mary Ann delos Santos, Archt. Jose Mari Cañizares of Cañizares Architects with Mr. Isidoro Cañizares, and Engr. Ricky Dakay of Dakay Construction and Development Corporation.

Base Line Center is strategically located at Cebu’s prime commercial district and sits in an iconic location that invokes memories that span four decades. It is just within 1 kilometer radius from major schools, universities, and just a walking distance from Redemptorist Church. Accessibility is at its most convenient with direct links to major roads, the Osmeña Blvd., Escario St., and Mango Avenue.

Base Line Center is a two phase project with four high-rise buildings. Phase 1 includes Base Line HQ (office) and Citadines Cebu City (serviced residences) in Tower 1 and Base Line Premier (residential) in Tower 2. Citadines Cebu City is managed by The Ascott Limited, the world’s number one serviced residences owner-operator. With the showroom finally open, clients and agents can drop by any time between 8:30 AM – 5:30 PM on Mondays to Fridays, and 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM on Saturdays for viewing and reservations.
